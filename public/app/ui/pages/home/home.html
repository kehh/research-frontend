The items embraced by these records have been collected and managed by the Society since 1926 and include a wealth of information on many aspects of Western Australian history dating back to European settlement in 1826, with some going back even further.

The catalogues cover the Library, Museum, Photographs and Public Memorials collections. You can also search across the collections. These catalogues have been made accessible via the web so that users can search them from anywhere in the world for historical research and general interest purposes.

The online Photographs collection catalogue is not complete. If you cannot find an image you are looking for, please contact the Library.
